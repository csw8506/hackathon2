What is Eco-Tourism?

Eco-Tourism is more than just sightseeing — it’s about traveling responsibly, preserving nature, and connecting with local communities. It promotes sustainability by supporting local economies and protecting the environment for future generations.

Top Attractions

Setouchi Festival

The festival of Setouchi is celebrated rarely, and all the people in the community share their thoughts and worship the gods together.

Okayama Castle

This is one of the best places to visit. The place is very clean and surrounded by the sea and green forest.

Ushimado

A beautiful sea where you can relax and enjoy the view. Perfect for swimming with family and friends during summer.

Culture

地域文化

訪問者に最適な歴史的な場所。瀬戸内と岡山の歴史を表現しています。

アートフェスティバル

地元の人々が作った手作りアート。瀬戸内トリエンナーレで現代アート展を楽しめます。

歴史的な寺院

非常に有名な岡山城を探索できます。訪問者は城の独特な建築を楽しめます。

Travel Tips

  • 🚆 Transport: Use the JR Pass for trains between islands.
  • 🌸 Best Time: Spring (cherry blossoms) or Autumn (mild weather).
  • 🚴 Activity: Cycle the Shimanami Kaido route.

Eco-Tourism Ideas

Low-impact adventures that support conservation and communities.

Shōdoshima

Olive & Citrus Farm Stays

Harvest with locals and learn water-wise farming on the ‘island of olives’.

Island Hopping

Low-Emission Boat Tours

Choose electric or solar boats between art islands and fishing towns.

Shimanami Kaido

Guided Cycling

Car-free routes, local guides, and plastic-free picnics with island produce.

Conservation

Reef & Shoreline Care

Join volunteer days restoring habitats and cleaning remote beaches.
